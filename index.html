About Me

Hello, my name is Kiana Tristan, I am 17 years old and as you can see I really love music. This portfolio that I put together are some of the things that includes music and I love it very much! I enjoy it, get into it, and dance like nobody's watching.

Concerts, microphones, and headphones are the things I love the most in music. When I go to concerts, all I hear are the voicesand the music behind it and it feels amazing. Now with microphones, I love singing to a lot of songs and I love how I get really into it and get to move to it, like I am giving a performance. Now lastly, headphones for me make me feel like I'm in my own world. Once those headphones are on my ears, then my song plays, I feel like it's just me and nobody else is around, and I really enjoy it.
